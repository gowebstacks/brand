# Brand Identity

## Mission
Webstacks builds high-performance websites and digital experiences for B2B SaaS companies. We combine strategic design with modern engineering to help teams ship faster and convert better.

## Brand Personality
- **Confident** — We lead with authority and expertise
- **Modern** — We embrace cutting-edge technology and design trends
- **Clear** — We communicate with precision, no fluff
- **Energetic** — We move fast and create momentum

## Voice & Tone
- Professional but approachable — not corporate-speak
- Direct and action-oriented — lead with verbs
- Technical when appropriate — our audience is savvy
- Avoid superlatives and hyperbole — let results speak

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise (under 25 words)
- Prefer concrete numbers over vague claims
- Use "we" when speaking as Webstacks, "you" when addressing the reader
- Headline style: sentence case, not title case

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
The Webstacks logo consists of the icon mark and wordmark. Use the full logo when space permits.

### Rules
- **Do**: Use on white, black, or brand teal backgrounds
- **Do**: Maintain minimum clear space equal to the icon height
- **Don't**: Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
- **Don't**: Place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- **Don't**: Recreate or modify the logo lockup

### Logo Colors
- On light backgrounds: icon in Blue (`--color-blue-600`), text in Black
- On dark backgrounds: icon in Blue (`--color-blue-600`), text in White
- Monochrome: all White on dark, all Black on light

## Brand Colors (Quick Reference)
See `colors.md` for the full palette. The three core brand colors are:
- **Teal** (primary brand) — `hsl(177, 94%, 13%)`
- **Blue** (UI primary / CTA) — `hsl(217, 89%, 46%)`
- **Violet** (tertiary) — `hsl(263, 94%, 19%)`
